Couples Massage Near Hale Mar

🌟 Suggestion: Schedule your couples massage for a mid-afternoon slot on a free day (Tue Jun 23 or Thu Jun 25 work great), then pair with dinner at Bite The Eye or Kaleo's nearby afterward.
✅ COUPLES ROOM · Top Pick
Mana Wellness Massage Therapy — Hilo
The best same-room couples massage option closest to Hale Mar — just 35 minutes away in downtown Hilo. They have a dedicated couples massage space where two therapists work side by side. Oceanfront location in the historic Hilo waterfront district. Swedish, Lomi Lomi (Hawaiian), Deep Tissue, Thai, and more available. Appointments available 7 days a week, walk-ins often welcome. Easy to combine with a Hilo dinner at Moon and Turtle or Pineapples after.
🗺 224 Kamehameha Ave, Ste 206, Hilo 📅 Book Online 🚗 ~35 min from Hale Mar 🕐 Daily 8 AM–7 PM 📞 (808) 747-7415

East Side Dining Near Hale Mar · 15–30 min

⭐ 4.4 · East Side Institution
Kaleo's Bar & Grill — Pāhoa & Keaau
The most beloved dinner spot on the east side — a true Big Island institution that won the Hale Aina Award for Best Big Island Restaurant. Open-air atmosphere with live Hawaiian music nightly. Fresh island fish prepared five ways, prime rib, steaks, kalua pork wontons, coconut shrimp, and the famous lilikoi cheesecake. Full bar with local draft beer and mango mai tais. Two locations: the original in Pahoa Village (~20 min) and a newer Keaau/Orchidland spot only 10 minutes from Hale Mar.
🗺 Pahoa: 15-2969 Pahoa Village Rd 🗺 Keaau: 16-110 Orchid Land Dr 📋 Menu 🚗 Pahoa ~20 min · Keaau ~10 min from Hale Mar 🕐 Pahoa: Thu–Sun 4–8 PM · Keaau: Daily 11 AM–8:30 PM 📞 (808) 965-5600
⭐ 4.8 · Casual Fave
Bite The Eye — Pāhoa
Your closest great restaurant — a beloved covered outdoor bar in Pahoa with fresh ahi burgers using local beef, Kahlua pork nachos, live music, and creative island cocktails. "Probably the best burger on the island." Happy hour 2–5 PM is 15% off everything. A local gem — casual, fun, and consistently excellent.
🗺 15-2059 Keaau-Pahoa Rd, Pāhoa 🚗 ~15 min from Hale Mar 🕐 Thu–Fri 2–8 PM · Sat 11 AM–9 PM · Sun 11 AM–6:30 PM · Closed Mon–Wed 📞 (808) 315-8710

Kona Restaurants ~2 hrs from Hale Mar

💡 Kona Dining Tip: On excursion days (Jun 20, 21, 22) you'll already be in Kona. These restaurants are perfect for adding a dinner before the drive home — especially sunset dinners on Aliʻi Drive.
⭐ 4.5 · Best Oceanfront
Magics Beach Grill
Arguably Kona's finest beachfront dining experience — waves crash into view from every table. Completely open-air. Watch the sunset over Magic Sands Beach while eating. Signature Ulu (Hawaiian breadfruit), beef bulgogi, lobster tail, rack of ribs. Arrive early or reserve — it fills up every night. Best sunset spot on Aliʻi Drive.
🗺 77-6452 Aliʻi Dr, Kailua-Kona 📋 Menu 🕐 Daily 8 AM–8:30 PM
⭐ 4.4 · Upscale Oceanfront
Kai Eats + Drinks
Refined oceanfront dining on Aliʻi Drive with exceptional fresh fish. Kampachi sashimi, ono piccata, and Kona coffee cheesecake are standouts. Great cocktail and wine program. One of the more elevated dining experiences in Kona without being stuffy.
🗺 75-5776 Aliʻi Dr, Kailua-Kona 📋 Menu 🕐 Mon–Fri 12–9 PM · Sat–Sun 9 AM–9 PM
⭐ 4.6 · Live Music
Happiness Kona
Fun waterfront bar and restaurant on Aliʻi Drive with a resident house band. Ocean views, creative tropical cocktails, and a Big Kalua Burger featuring beef, kalua pork, Portuguese sausage, pineapple, and shrimp. Great energy — one of the liveliest spots in Kona for an evening out.
🗺 75-5699 Aliʻi Dr #201, Kona 📋 Menu 🕐 Wed–Thu 11 AM–3 PM, 5–9 PM · Fri–Sat 11 AM–11 PM · Sun 9 AM–2 PM
⭐ 4.3 · Casual Waterfront
Papa Kona Restaurant & Bar
Casual oceanfront spot on the Waterfront Row — great for a relaxed lunch or pre-activity dinner. Poke tower, fish tacos, volcano fries with kalua pork, and excellent mai tais. Parking validated in the garage below. A solid, fun Kona staple.
🗺 Waterfront Row, 75-5770 Aliʻi Dr 📋 Menu 🕐 Daily 7 AM–8 PM 📞 (808) 300-0044

Hilo Restaurants ~35–40 min from Hale Mar

⭐ 4.7 · Reserve NOW
Moon and Turtle
One of Hilo's absolute best restaurants — perhaps the finest food on the east side of the Big Island. Inventive Pacific Rim cuisine with the freshest local fish and produce. Smoky sashimi, sizzling poke, mushroom risotto, craft desserts. Only about 10 tables — this place is tiny and extremely popular with locals. Book before you leave for Hawaii. "Stop whatever you're doing and call now."
🗺 51 Kalakaua St, Hilo 📋 Menu 🕐 Tue–Sat 5:30–9 PM · Closed Sun–Mon 📞 (808) 961-0599
⚠️ Only ~10 tables — reservations essential. Book before your trip!
⭐ 4.7 · Planetarium Dining
Lehua Restaurant — Imiloa Astronomy Center
An unexpected gem inside Hilo's Imiloa Astronomy Center / planetarium. Exceptional farm-to-table cooking with Japanese and Hawaiian influences. Live Hawaiian music most evenings. Reviewers drive 1.5 hours from Kona just for this meal. Try the pickled mango martini, pork belly bowl, and dessert lumpia. Genuinely one of the best meals on the island.
🗺 600 Imiloa Pl, Hilo 📋 Menu 🕐 Lunch Wed–Sun · Dinner daily 5–9 PM 📞 (808) 746-2226
⭐ 4.6 · Local Favorite
Pineapples Island Fresh Cuisine
One of Hilo's most beloved restaurants — fresh local seafood at fair prices with live music and great service. The calamari (panko, perfectly tender), fresh grilled catch, and Hilo Plate are standouts. Much easier to get a table than Moon and Turtle. Great for the whole family.
🗺 332 Keawe St, Hilo 📋 Menu 🕐 Tue–Sun 11 AM–9 PM · Closed Mon 📞 (808) 238-5324
